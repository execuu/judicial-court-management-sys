Judicial Court Management System –

A case-tracking system that registers cases, assigns judges and lawyers,
schedules hearings, and manages evidence. It simulates the judicial workflow
from filing to resolution, ensuring records are maintained and reports generated
efficiently.
• Register cases and assign case numbers.
• Assign judges and court dates.
• Track evidence and documents.
• Manage lawyer assignments.
• Record hearing results.
• Handle scheduling conflicts.
• Generate case progress reports.
• Archive closed cases.
